BFRP Fiber
BasaltechSA Fiber
An innovative reinforcing material derived from volcanic basalt rock that dramatically improves concrete properties. High tensile strength, total corrosion resistance, and outstanding durability.
Applications
Road Surfaces Reinforces asphalt and cement concrete roads, runways, and pavements.
Industrial Floors Ideal for logistics centres, parking lots, helipads, and screeds.
Underground & Civil Structures Enhances durability in metros, underground foundations, and communications.
Buildings & Bridges Strengthens structural elements, concrete structures, and dry mix applications.
Soil Stabilisation Improves ground stability for slopes, embankments, and foundations.
